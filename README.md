LeadScore Pro: Multi-Tenant AI Revenue IntelligenceLeadScore Pro is a production-grade, multi-tenant AI platform designed to help sales and marketing teams identify high-value leads through a sophisticated Dual-Model Architecture. By fusing firmographic "Fit" with real-time "Buyer Intent," the system transforms raw behavioral data into actionable sales intelligence.
The Dual-Model ApproachUnlike traditional static scoring, LeadScore Pro evaluates leads through two distinct lenses to bridge the gap between "who" a lead is and "when" they are ready to buy:The Conversion Model (The "Who"): An ensemble model (XGBoost, RandomForest, Logistic Regression) that analyzes stable data like company size, industry, job role, and lead source to determine long-term fit.The Intent Model (The "When"): A specialized XGBoost model focused on high-velocity behavioral signals, such as pricing page visits, demo requests, and product comparisons.Score Fusion: The final score is a weighted blend (defaulting to 60% Fit / 40% Intent), allowing teams to prioritize leads who are not just a good match, but are actively looking to buy now.
Key FeaturesMulti-Tenant Architecture: Built for SaaS with hard data isolation, tenant-specific model artifacts, and scoped API keys.Real-Time Event Pipeline: Processes incoming event streams (via Segment, HubSpot, or webhooks) to re-score leads instantly upon high-priority actions like demo requests.Advanced Feature Engineering: Implements exponential recency decay ($\lambda = 14$ days), engagement velocity, and complex sequence detection (e.g., Case Study View $\rightarrow$ Pricing Page $\rightarrow$ Demo Request).Automated Training DAGs: Orchestrated retraining pipelines using Airflow to evaluate "Champion vs. Challenger" models, ensuring the highest predictive accuracy over time.Explainable AI (XAI): Provides human-readable "Reason Codes" and SHAP attribution to show sales reps exactly why a lead reached their current score.
Tech StackBackend: FastAPI, Python (Scikit-Learn, XGBoost, Pandas).Frontend: React (Dashboard with real-time intent visualization).Data/Infrastructure: PostgreSQL (Multi-tenant schema), Airflow (Training DAGs), Docker.Integrations: HubSpot, Salesforce, Segment, and Google Ads.
